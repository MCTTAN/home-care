Health literacy is invaluable.


Without health literacy, accessing health information and services can lead to putting personal health at risk. The U.S. Department of Health and Human Services reports that as many as 9 in 10 adults have limited health literacy.

Health communication plays a major role in furthering health literacy. Today's Home Care advocates for our nurses and aides to present patients with information in a variety of digestible ways, which helps families with self-management and informed decision-making.
